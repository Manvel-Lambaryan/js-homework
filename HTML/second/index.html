Life and Struggle (Kyanq u Kriv)

Overview

"Life and Struggle" is an Armenian drama film that follows the intertwined destinies of several young friends as their lives are dramatically altered by the outbreak of the Nagorno-Karabakh War. The story begins in the late 1980s, showcasing their everyday lives, dreams, and relationships before the conflict. As the war intensifies, the friends are called to the front lines, where they must confront the brutal realities of combat, sacrifice, and survival. The film masterfully weaves together their personal stories of love, loss, and courage against the backdrop of a nation's struggle.

Main Characters

Edgar

Edgar is the film's main protagonist, a talented and sensitive young man whose life is forever changed by the war. Initially focused on his future and his relationship with his girlfriend, he reluctantly joins the military, only to become a courageous and steadfast soldier. Edgar's journey is a powerful exploration of how an ordinary person can find extraordinary strength and leadership in the face of immense adversity.

Lilit

Lilit is Edgar's girlfriend and a symbol of the love and hope that the war threatens to extinguish. She represents the civilians left behind, enduring the hardships and uncertainty of the conflict from a distance. Lilit's character provides a crucial perspective on the emotional toll of war on families and loved ones, showcasing her resilience and unwavering support for those on the front lines.
